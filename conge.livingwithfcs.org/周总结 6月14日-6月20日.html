So when happiness arrives, I will be healthy enough to enjoy it. That's the current plan.<br /><br />To live, to Love, to Learn and to leave a legacy... These are what people to to bring happiness to him/herself. I am doing the first daily, the second several times a week, the third and the last one barely. I need to find the mission of my life to lead my out of the daily conflicts I am struggling with, inside and outside.<br /><br />写着写着又回到硬牙膏了，还写成了语句不通的英文。不做修改了，就这样吧。
