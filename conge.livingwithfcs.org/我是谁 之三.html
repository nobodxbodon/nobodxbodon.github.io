仓库源文，站点原文

<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small; "><br>【原文】<br>Ego arises when your sense of Beingness, of "I Am," which is formless consciousness, gets mixed up with form. This is the meaning of identification. This is forgetfulness of Being, the primary error, the illusion of absolute separateness that turns reality into a nightmare.<br><br>Ego is always identification with form, seeking yourself and thereby losing yourself in some form. Forms are not just material objects and physical bodies. More fundamental than the external forms —— things and bodies——are the thought forms that continuously arise in the field of consciousness.</span><div><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small; "><br>【译文】<br>自我的出现，来自于人对存在或者“我存在”<wbr/>这些无形之物的觉知与具体的形态的混淆。<wbr/>自我是认同感，也是对存在的遗忘。这些导致事物是绝对分离的幻觉。这个致命的错误，<wbr/>将现实弄得一塌糊涂。<br><br>自我总是寻求与各种具体形态的认同，这种寻找必将导致你强调某些形态，却忽视其他一些。形态并非仅是物质。<wbr/>比那些外在的形态——也即各种事物——更基本的，<wbr/>是以思维形式存在的形态。他们在意识中不停闪现。</span></div><div><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small; "><br>【心得】<br>如果有人对你说，想法是物体，你能接受么?我想我能。我们不能“<wbr/>看见”想法，也无法听到，但是当它出现时，你知道。</span><div><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small; ">我们不知道想法是怎么出现的，<wbr/>但是它总是不停的冒出来——尤其是在你无所事事的时候。<wbr/>如果你能接受思维是事物，是形态，就可以了。</span></div><div><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small; ">这一段除了这个，<wbr/>没有将别的额外意思。</span></div></div>
