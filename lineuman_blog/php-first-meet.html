2.php header破除同源限制，实现便捷调试

<?php
/*注意顺序哦,header要在echo之前进行调用*/
header('Content-Type:text/html;charset=utf-8');
header('Access-Control-Allow-Origin:*');

echo "<html>";
echo "<h1>this is for test</h1>";
echo "</html>";
?>

These superglobal variables are
$GLOBALS
$_SERVER
$_GET
$_POST
$_FILE
$_COOKIE
$_SESSION
